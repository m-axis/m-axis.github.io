# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 25, 2026

**App name:** Dot = Language  
**Developer:** Meditation Games  
**Package name:** `com.dotsequals.language`  
**Current version:** 1.0.0

Meditation Games (“we,” “us,” or “our”) built Dot = Language as a learning game to help users practice Hindi, Tamil, and English through puzzles and mini-games. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and what choices you have.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

---

## Summary

Dot = Language is designed to work **primarily on your device**. We do **not** operate user accounts, advertising networks, or analytics services inside the app. We do **not** sell personal information. Most data stays on your phone or tablet in local app storage.

---

## Information we collect

### Information you provide

- **Display name (optional):** You may enter a name in Settings. If you leave it blank, the app shows “User.” This name is stored only on your device and is used for greetings and your progress summary in Settings.

### Information collected automatically on your device

The app stores **game and settings data locally** using Android DataStore, including for example:

- Selected learning language (Hindi, Tamil, or English)
- Game progress (such as current level, lesson indexes, alphabet position, sentence index)
- Coins, learning points, and badge progress
- Shop purchases and equipped items (themes, dot styles, shapes, line colors, emoji stickers, celebration styles, flairs, mascots, and similar in-app unlocks)
- Your chosen app theme and cosmetic preferences

This information is used to run the game and restore your progress when you reopen the app.

### Information we do not collect

We do **not** intentionally collect:

- Email addresses, phone numbers, or passwords (there is no sign-in)
- Precise location
- Contacts, photos, microphone recordings, or camera images
- Payment card details (the app uses an in-game coin system only; any real-money purchases would be handled by Google Play, not inside our app code)

---

## How we use information

We use locally stored data only to:

- Provide gameplay, hints, rewards, and the Fun Shop
- Remember your language choice and cosmetic settings
- Show your optional name and progress report in Settings
- Improve stability and correct bugs when you report issues to us

We do **not** use your data for targeted advertising or cross-app tracking.

---

## Text-to-speech

The app uses Android’s **built-in text-to-speech (TTS)** to read words and letters aloud in Connect, Alphabet, Sentences, and related modes. Speech is processed by the **TTS engine installed on your device** (for example, Google Text-to-Speech). We do not receive audio recordings from this feature. Behavior may depend on your device manufacturer and language packs you have installed.

---

## Network and third parties

### Internet access

The core app does **not** require an internet connection to play. We do not send your game progress or optional name to Meditation Games servers through the app itself.

### Third-party services

The app uses standard Android libraries (such as Jetpack Compose, Navigation, and DataStore) supplied with the app binary. We do not embed third-party advertising or analytics SDKs in the current version.

If you install or update the app through **Google Play**, Google’s own terms and privacy policy apply to that store and download process.

### Device backup

`android:allowBackup` is enabled. If you turn on **Android backup** (for example, Google backup on your device), your app data may be included in encrypted backups managed by your device or Google account according to your system settings. We do not control those backups directly.

---

## Children’s privacy

Dot = Language is intended for **young learners** and families. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is stored locally on the device (such as an optional first name chosen by a parent or child).

We encourage parents and guardians to:

- Supervise use of the app
- Avoid entering a child’s full legal name if you prefer; a nickname or “User” is fine
- Review device backup and TTS settings on the child’s device

If you believe a child has provided personal information to us in a way that concerns you, contact us (see below) and we will work with you to address it. Because data is stored locally, deleting the app or clearing its storage usually removes stored preferences on that device.

---

## Data retention and deletion

- Data remains on your device until you **clear app storage**, **uninstall** the app, or reset app data from Android Settings.
- Uninstalling Dot = Language removes local app data from that device (subject to any backups you may have enabled separately).

We do not retain copies of your on-device game data on our own servers for the current version of the app.

---

## Security

We use reasonable measures within the app design (local storage, no account system, no in-app analytics). No method of storage is 100% secure. Keep your device updated and use a screen lock appropriate for your family.

---

## Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information. For this app, you can generally:

- Change or clear your optional name in **Settings**
- Clear all app data via **Android Settings → Apps → Dot = Language → Storage → Clear storage**
- Uninstall the app

Because we do not operate a cloud account for this app, requests to access data we hold centrally may not apply; most data exists only on your device.

---

## International users

The app is offered globally. Local data stays on your device. If laws in your region grant additional privacy rights, you may contact us to exercise them where applicable.
